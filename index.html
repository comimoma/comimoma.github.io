About Me

Hi! I'm Connie Ou and welcome to my website 😉

I'm in love with both hardware design, software development, cooking, and traveling with my furry kids. I have been working in electrical engineering field for a few years and skilled with hardware design software like OrCAD, PADS Layout, Eagle, and Altium. While developing professional engineering skills both at school and workplace, I started getting more experience about software developement. I then gained some experience about setting up an Ubuntu Server from scratch, writing functional programs in python, JavaScript, JSON, and C#/C++ to help with hardware testing data collection and microchip programming. In addition, I built the MQTT system and UI interface program for touchscreen using touchGFX by STMicroelectronics for our customers. Currently, I'm studying website development and various operating systems in order to get deeper in software design field. The next goal in my career is to become a freelance application designer in both hardware and software developement.

I welcame two cuties into my life in the year 2020, whose names are Mocha and Maple. Mocha is a snow bengal and he loves to talk all the time. He is also extremely friendly to everyone and loves to be pet. Unlike other in different cats, Mocha is totally a lap cat. He can even be recalled and shake paws! Maple is a mixbreed with half Bernese Mountain Dog and half standard poodle, which is so called Bernedoodle or Bernerdoodle. She is a lovely and reckless girl who always gets incredibly wet and muddy outside. She is similar to Mocha that she greets everyone on the street during the walks. Maple and I have been in many training sections and she is now qualified to become a therapy dog. Both of us are excited to join the volunteer screw soon and start our journey of bring others love and happiness. Mocha and Maple are the best company during pandemic and make my daily life with uncountable fun.
